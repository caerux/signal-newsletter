The summary
  • First OpenAI model with reasoning baked into the base weights — no separate "o-series" branch.

Why it matters
This isn't a model release — it's a repricing of intelligence. Every AI startup's cost structure changed overnight.
The Takeaway
If your product is a GPT-4 wrapper, you have thirty days to justify your margin — or ship something only a reasoning-native model can do.
